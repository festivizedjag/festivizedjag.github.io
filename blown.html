Humble Beginnings: Blown To Bits Presentation

GRADE RECIEVED: 4/A
FINISHED ON: AUGUST 14,2019
PEOPLE INVOLVED: JHUN BACLAAN, CORY PARKER, PRESTON CORPUZ, ETHAN MORRELL, AND MONIKA ALLEN

For our second project, we were set into groups by numbers 1-6. Each group was designated to read a chapter from the book
"Blown to Bits". My group was tasked with chapter two, and the project was due on August 22, 2019. The first and second day of the project consisted of
reading the entire chapter of the book and gathering ideas for slides.

After we created the slideshow, we presented the entire thing to the class.
Each slideshow was required to have no more than 9 slides, each with no more than 8 bullets.
